About Us

Brightlane Logistics is at the cutting edge of the transportation industry by enhancing proven methods and developing new ones to bring back the reliability to logistics. We pride ourself in having strong connections with carriers, shippers and industry experts to truly provide a high level of service for any of your shipping needs.

Capacity

With fewer trucks on the road and an increase of products being shipped. It is more important than ever to have a strong connection with the trucking community. Brightlane prides itself in its partnerships with small and large carriers to transport your loads anywhere in the USA at a time notice.

Rates

With rates constantly fluctuating Brightalne Logistics stays ahead by constantly monitoring market patterns as well as having direct contact with carriers who constantly run those lanes to give our clients a true fair market rate.

Dedicated Team

Teamwork in this industry is key to providing exceptional service. From load tender to invoice our dedicated agents are knowledgable and have a clear understanding of what it takes to get the job done.

Services

Dry

Are one of the most common type of loads in the industry, at brightlane logistics we understand that every load has its particular guidelines that are required so that product is moved with care. From automotive parts to dry foods we find the correct equipment to get your products moving.

Reefer

From frozen to fresh produce, dairy and meats we maintain constant communication with our carriers to assure that correct temperature is maintained throughout its transit until its final destination.

Flatbed/Oversized

We specialize in loads that don't follow conventional trailer dimensions and weight. We have experience in moving industrial equipment, bulky material and other oversized loads. From getting the correct permits to having the correct tarps we make sure the job gets done.

Intermodal

We offer sustained capacity and rates in a tough lane or Peak season through our intermodal connections. This benefits our clients with loads traveling long distances and large volumes.
